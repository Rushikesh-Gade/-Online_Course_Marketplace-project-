Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your learning experience

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

LearnHub offers a 30-day full refund policy. If you are not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase, provided you have completed less than 30% of the course content.

How to Request a Refund

Go to your Student Dashboard → My Courses → select the course → click "Request Refund". Fill in the reason and submit. Our team will review your request within 2 business days.

Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed within 5 business days and returned to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, or PayPal).

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued if more than 30% of course content has been completed, or if the refund request is made after 30 days of purchase.

Need Help?

Contact us at support@learnhub.com for any refund-related queries.
