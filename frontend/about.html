About LearnHub

Our mission is to make quality education accessible to every learner, everywhere

What is LearnHub?

LearnHub is an online course marketplace that connects passionate instructors with eager learners. We believe that education should be affordable, flexible, and effective — regardless of where you live or what your background is.

Built as a final year major project, LearnHub demonstrates how modern web technologies can be combined with AI and real-time communication to create a truly next-generation learning platform.

What sets us apart is our focus on community-driven learning — with Virtual Study Rooms for peer collaboration and an AI Study Assistant that helps students understand concepts instantly, right inside the course.
