What's the difference between async/await and .then() chaining?

Rishi Patil (Instructor)

Both achieve the same result but async/await is more readable and easier to debug. Use await when you have sequential operations and .then() when chaining is simpler.

Can you explain async/await in simple words?

Sure! Imagine you order food at a restaurant. Instead of standing at the counter waiting (blocking), you sit down and do other things. When food is ready, you're notified. That's async/await — your code continues while waiting for a task to finish, then resumes when it's done.
