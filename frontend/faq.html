Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about LearnHub

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse courses, click on your preferred course, and click "Enroll Now". You can pay securely using a credit/debit card or PayPal. Once payment is confirmed, you get instant access.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. LearnHub offers a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you have completed less than 30% of the course, you can request a full refund through the Refund Portal in your dashboard.

How does the Virtual Study Room work?

Every course has a dedicated Virtual Study Room. Enrolled students can join to study together in real time, share notes, chat, and use the built-in focus timer to stay productive.

What is the AI Study Assistant?

The AI Study Assistant is a built-in chatbot powered by Google Gemini AI. While watching a lecture, you can ask it to explain concepts, give examples, or quiz you on what you've learned.

How do I become an instructor?

Sign up for a free account, go to your dashboard settings, and apply for Instructor access. Once approved by our admin team, you can start creating and publishing courses.

When do instructors get paid?

Instructors receive 70% of the course revenue. Payouts are processed once per month and transferred directly to your registered bank account or PayPal within 7 business days.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes! Upon completing all lessons in a course, LearnHub automatically generates a verified certificate of completion that you can download and share on LinkedIn or your resume.
